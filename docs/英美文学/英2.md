## 二、单选题

**1. (单选题) In the English Renaissance period, scholars began to emphasize the capacities of the human mind and the achievements of human culture. The most significant intellectual movement was ________.**
A. reformation
B. geographical explorations
C. humanism
D. the Italian revival
*   **正确答案：** C
*   **解析：** 文艺复兴时期最重要的思想运动是人文主义 (humanism)，强调人的价值和能力。

**2. (单选题) Which of the following plays does not belong to Shakespeare's great tragedies?**
A. Othello
B. Macbeth
C. Romeo and Juliet
D. Hamlet
*   **正确答案：** C
*   **解析：** 莎士比亚四大悲剧是《哈姆雷特》《奥赛罗》《李尔王》《麦克白》，《罗密欧与朱丽叶》虽为悲剧但不属于四大悲剧。

**3. (单选题) Which of the following plays does not belong to Shakespeare's great comedies?**
A. The Comedy of Errors
B. The Merchant of Venice
C. A Midsummer Night's Dream
D. As You Like It
*   **正确答案：** A
*   **解析：** 莎士比亚四大喜剧通常指《仲夏夜之梦》《威尼斯商人》《皆大欢喜》《第十二夜》，《错误的喜剧》不在其中。

**4. (单选题) Which is not the works of Christopher Marlowe?**
A. Lycidas
B. Tamburlaine
C. The Jew of Malta
D. Doctor Faustus
*   **正确答案：** A
*   **解析：** 《利西达斯》(Lycidas) 是约翰·弥尔顿的作品，不是马洛的作品。

**5. (单选题) Which of the following poetic forms is the principal form of Shakespeare's dramas?**
A. lyric
B. sonnet
C. blank verse
D. quatrain
*   **正确答案：** C
*   **解析：** 莎士比亚戏剧的主要诗歌形式是无韵诗 (blank verse)，即不押韵的五步抑扬格。

**6. (单选题) Of the following plays, which is not written by Ben Jonson?**
A. Every Man in His Humor
B. Volpone
C. The Alchemist
D. The Shoemaker's Holiday
*   **正确答案：** D
*   **解析：** 《鞋匠的节日》(The Shoemaker's Holiday) 是托马斯·德克 (Thomas Dekker) 的作品。

**7. (单选题) "Some books are to be tasted, others to be swallowed, and some few to be chewed and digested" is one of the epigrams found in ________.**
A. Francis Bacon's "Of Studies"
B. Thomas More's Utopia
C. John Bunyan's The Pilgrim's Progress
D. Shakespeare's Othello
*   **正确答案：** A
*   **解析：** 这句名言出自弗朗西斯·培根的散文《论读书》(Of Studies)。

**8. (单选题) Francis Bacon is best known for his ____ which greatly influenced the development of this literary form.**
A. poems
B. essays
C. plays
D. epics
*   **正确答案：** B
*   **解析：** 培根以他的散文 (essays) 闻名，对英国散文这一文学形式的发展产生了重大影响。

**9. (单选题) Paradise Lost is actually a story taken from ____.**
A. the Renaissance
B. the Old Testament
C. Greek Mythology
D. the New Testament
*   **正确答案：** B
*   **解析：** 《失乐园》的故事取自《旧约圣经》，讲述撒旦反叛以及亚当和夏娃被逐出伊甸园的故事。

**10. (单选题) "The Fairy Queen" is the masterpiece written by ____.**
A. John Milton
B. Geoffrey Chaucer
C. Alexander Pope
D. Edmund Spenser
*   **正确答案：** D
*   **解析：** 《仙后》(The Faerie Queene) 是埃德蒙·斯宾塞的代表作。

## 三、填空题

**1. (填空题) In ____ the English navy defeated the Spanish Armada.**
*   **正确答案：** 1588
*   **解析：** 1588年，英国海军击败了西班牙无敌舰队。

**2. (填空题) Absolute monarchy in England reached its summit during the reign of ____ because he carried out the ________.**
*   **正确答案：** Henry VIII; English Reformation
*   **解析：** 亨利八世推行宗教改革，使英国君主专制达到顶峰。

**3. (填空题) The ________ in 1688 established the ________ monarchy.**
*   **正确答案：** Glorious Revolution; constitutional
*   **解析：** 1688年的光荣革命 (Glorious Revolution) 确立了君主立宪制 (constitutional monarchy)。

**4. (填空题) Paradise Lost tells how ____ rebelled against God and how ____ and Eve were driven out of Eden.**
*   **正确答案：** Satan; Adam
*   **解析：** 《失乐园》讲述撒旦反叛上帝以及亚当和夏娃被逐出伊甸园的故事。

**5. (填空题) ________ was the religious doctrine of the revolutionary bourgeoisie during the English Revolution.**
*   **正确答案：** Puritanism
*   **解析：** 清教主义 (Puritanism) 是英国革命时期革命资产阶级的宗教信条。
