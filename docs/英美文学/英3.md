## 二、填空题

**1. (填空题) In the 18th century, social life developed rapidly, ________, each as a social centre, sprang up in London, and became the most striking feature of London life.**
*   **正确答案：** coffee houses
*   **解析：** 18世纪伦敦兴起了许多咖啡馆 (coffee houses)，成为社交中心和伦敦生活最显著的特征。

**2. (填空题) The 18th century is known as "the Age of ____".**
*   **正确答案：** Enlightenment / Reason
*   **解析：** 18世纪被称为"启蒙时代" (the Age of Enlightenment) 或"理性的时代"。

**3. (填空题) The founder of the English realistic school is ________.**
*   **正确答案：** Daniel Defoe
*   **解析：** 丹尼尔·笛福被认为是英国现实主义流派的奠基人。

**4. (填空题) Such beautiful and enduring lines as "And I will luve thee still, my dear, Till a' the seas gang dry" were written by ________ in his famous love poem ________.**
*   **正确答案：** Robert Burns; "A Red, Red Rose"
*   **解析：** 这些诗句出自罗伯特·彭斯的名诗《一朵红红的玫瑰》。

**5. (填空题) Daniel Defoe describes ____ as a typical English middle-class man of the eighteenth century, the very prototype of the empire builder or the pioneer colonist.**
*   **正确答案：** Robinson Crusoe
*   **解析：** 笛福将鲁滨逊·克鲁索塑造为18世纪典型的英国中产阶级人物，殖民开拓者的原型。

## 三、单选题

**1. (单选题) The ________ was a progressive intellectual movement throughout the western Europe in the 18th century.**
A. Renaissance
B. Religious Reformation
C. Enlightenment
D. Chartist Movement
*   **正确答案：** C
*   **解析：** 启蒙运动 (Enlightenment) 是18世纪西欧的一场进步思想运动。

**2. (单选题) In the 18th Century neo-classic English literature, the best representative poet of neo-classicism is ________.**
A. Alexander Pope
B. Daniel Defoe
C. Matthew Arnold
D. John Milton
*   **正确答案：** A
*   **解析：** 亚历山大·蒲柏 (Alexander Pope) 是英国新古典主义最杰出的代表诗人。

**3. (单选题) Of all the romantic poets in the 18th century, ________ is the most independent and the most original.**
A. Thomas Gray
B. William Blake
C. Alexander Pope
D. Daniel Defoe
*   **正确答案：** B
*   **解析：** 威廉·布莱克 (William Blake) 是18世纪浪漫主义诗人中最独立、最具独创性的一位。

**4. (单选题) Blake's Songs of Experience paints a world of ____ with a melancholy tone.**
A. misery, poverty, disease, war and repression
B. happiness and love and romantic ideals
C. misery, poverty mixed with love and happiness
D. loss and institutional cruelty with sufferings
*   **正确答案：** A
*   **解析：** 布莱克的《经验之歌》以忧郁的笔调描绘了一个充满苦难、贫穷、疾病、战争和压迫的世界。

**5. (单选题) Robert Burns is the representative of ____.**
A. Sentimentalism
B. Pre-Romanticism
C. Romanticism
D. English Renaissance
*   **正确答案：** B
*   **解析：** 罗伯特·彭斯是前浪漫主义 (Pre-Romanticism) 的代表人物。

**6. (单选题) Robinson Crusoe can be termed as ____.**
A. a self-dependent person
B. a person with colonial mind
C. an adventurous person
D. all of the above
*   **正确答案：** D
*   **解析：** 鲁滨逊·克鲁索集自立、殖民精神和冒险精神于一身。

**7. (单选题) The literature of the Enlightenment in England mainly appealed to the ____ readers.**
A. aristocratic
B. middle class
C. low class
D. intellectual
*   **正确答案：** B
*   **解析：** 英国启蒙文学主要面向中产阶级读者。

**8. (单选题) The most remarkable achievement in the 18th century English literature is ________.**
A. drama
B. poetry
C. novel
D. essay
*   **正确答案：** C
*   **解析：** 18世纪英国文学最显著的成就是小说 (novel) 的兴起与发展。

**9. (单选题) "Proper words in proper places, makes the true definition of a style." This sentence is said by ____, one of the greatest masters of English prose.**
A. Alexander Pope
B. Henry Fielding
C. Daniel Defoe
D. Jonathan Swift
*   **正确答案：** D
*   **解析：** 这句名言出自乔纳森·斯威夫特 (Jonathan Swift)，他是英国散文的大师。

**10. (单选题) ____ is not written by William Blake.**
A. Songs of Experience
B. Poems, Chiefly in the Scottish Dialect
C. Songs of Innocence
D. Poetical Sketches
*   **正确答案：** B
*   **解析：** 《主要用苏格兰方言写的诗集》(Poems, Chiefly in the Scottish Dialect) 是罗伯特·彭斯的作品，不是布莱克的。
