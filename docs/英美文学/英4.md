## 二、填空题

**1. (填空题) Romanticism in literature was exclusively motivated by both the ____ and the ____.**
*   **正确答案：** French Revolution; Industrial Revolution
*   **解析：** 文学上的浪漫主义受到法国大革命和工业革命的双重推动。

**2. (填空题) The publication of ____ marked the beginning of Romanticism.**
*   **正确答案：** Lyrical Ballads
*   **解析：** 《抒情歌谣集》(Lyrical Ballads, 1798) 的出版标志着浪漫主义的开始。

**3. (填空题) The death of ____ marked the ending of Romanticism.**
*   **正确答案：** Sir Walter Scott
*   **解析：** 1832年司各特 (Sir Walter Scott) 的去世标志着英国浪漫主义时期的结束。

**4. (填空题) Jane Austen's novels are about ____ and ____.**
*   **正确答案：** love; marriage
*   **解析：** 简·奥斯汀的小说主题围绕爱情和婚姻展开。

**5. (填空题) Owing to difference in political attitudes, the romanticists split into two schools: ____ romanticists and ____ romanticists.**
*   **正确答案：** active / revolutionary; passive / conservative
*   **解析：** 由于政治态度不同，浪漫主义者分为积极（革命）浪漫主义者和消极（保守）浪漫主义者两派。

## 三、单选题

**1. (单选题) William Wordsworth, a romantic poet, advocated all of the following EXCEPT ____.**
A. Normal contemporary speech patterns
B. Humble and rustic life as subject matter
C. Elegant wording and inflated figures of speech
D. Intensely subjective feeling toward individual experience
*   **正确答案：** C
*   **解析：** 华兹华斯主张使用普通人的日常语言，反对华丽的措辞和夸张的修辞手法。

**2. (单选题) "If winter comes, can spring be far behind?" is taken from ____.**
A. The Solitary Reaper
B. Ode to the West Wind
C. To Autumn
D. Song to the Man of England
*   **正确答案：** B
*   **解析：** "冬天来了，春天还会远吗？"出自雪莱的《西风颂》(Ode to the West Wind)。

**3. (单选题) ____ is NOT among the representative essayists in the romantic times.**
A. Charles Lamb
B. William Hazlitt
C. Thomas De Quincey
D. Walter Scott
*   **正确答案：** D
*   **解析：** 司各特 (Walter Scott) 是历史小说家，不是散文家；兰姆、哈兹里特和德·昆西都是浪漫主义时期著名的散文家。

**4. (单选题) In ____, ____ set forth his principles of poetry, "all good poetry is the spontaneous overflow of powerful feeling".**
A. The Preface to Lyrical Ballads; Wordsworth
B. "The Rime of the Ancient Mariner"; Coleridge
C. "A Defence of Poetry"; Shelley
D. "Lectures on the English Poets"; Hazlitt
*   **正确答案：** A
*   **解析：** 华兹华斯在《抒情歌谣集·序言》中提出"一切好诗都是强烈情感的自然流露"。

**5. (单选题) Keats wrote five great odes in 1819. ____ is NOT among them.**
A. Ode to a Nightingale
B. Ode to the West Wind
C. Ode to Autumn
D. Ode on Indolence
*   **正确答案：** B
*   **解析：** 《西风颂》是雪莱的作品，济慈1819年的五大颂歌包括《夜莺颂》《秋颂》《怠惰颂》《希腊古瓮颂》《忧郁颂》。

**6. (单选题) Coleridge's masterpiece is ____.**
A. The Prelude
B. Kubla Khan
C. The Rime of the Ancient Mariner
D. Tintern Abbey
*   **正确答案：** C
*   **解析：** 《古舟子咏》(The Rime of the Ancient Mariner) 是柯勒律治的代表作。

**7. (单选题) All the following are novels written by Jane Austen EXCEPT ____.**
A. Mansfield Park
B. Shirley
C. Emma
D. Persuasion
*   **正确答案：** B
*   **解析：** 《谢利》(Shirley) 是夏洛蒂·勃朗特的小说，不是简·奥斯汀的作品。

**8. (单选题) At the beginning of Pride and Prejudice, the attitude of Darcy and Elizabeth toward each other is that of ____.**
A. mutual affection
B. mutual repulsion
C. mutual hatred
D. mutual indifference
*   **正确答案：** B
*   **解析：** 在《傲慢与偏见》开头，达西和伊丽莎白彼此反感 (mutual repulsion)，分别体现为傲慢与偏见。

**9. (单选题) The Romantic Movement expressed a ____ attitude toward the existing social and political conditions that came with industrialization and the growing importance of the bourgeoisie.**
A. negative
B. neutral
C. positive
D. indifferent
*   **正确答案：** A
*   **解析：** 浪漫主义运动对工业化及资产阶级崛起所带来的社会和政治现状持消极 (negative) 态度。
