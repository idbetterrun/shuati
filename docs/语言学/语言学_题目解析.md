# 一、选择题

## （一）Exercise 1 · 第一组（第1–10题）

**1. Modern linguistics differs from traditional grammar for it is mostly _______.**
A. prescriptive　B. descriptive　C. subjective　D. Latin-based
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：现代语言学是**描写性(descriptive)**的，只客观描写语言实际使用情况，不评判对错，故选 B。A. prescriptive（规定性）是传统语法的特征，规定"应该怎么说"，方向相反；C. subjective（主观的）与语言学追求客观科学的性质相悖；D. Latin-based（以拉丁语为基础）指传统语法套用拉丁语框架，也是传统语法而非现代语言学的特点。

**2. A linguist regards the changes in language and language use as ______.**
A. abnormal　B. something to be feared　C. natural　D. unnatural
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：语言学家视语言变化为**自然(natural)**现象，是语言的固有属性，故选 C。A. abnormal（反常的）、D. unnatural（不自然的）都否定语言变化的合理性，是规定主义者的态度；B. something to be feared（应当惧怕的）同样是保守的规定性观点，与语言学家的客观立场不符。

**3. Which of the following sounds is a voiced consonant?**
A. [d]　B. [t]　C. [p]　D. [t]
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：[d] 是**浊(voiced)**齿龈塞音，发音时声带振动，故选 A。B/D 的 [t] 是清齿龈塞音，C 的 [p] 是清双唇塞音，发音时声带都不振动，均为清音(voiceless)。

**4. There are ______ morphemes in the word "disabled".**
A. one　B. two　C. three　D. four
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：disabled 可切分为 **dis- + able + -ed** 三个语素，故选 C。dis-（否定前缀）、able（词根）、-ed（屈折后缀），共 3 个，其余选项数目不符。

**5. In English, "dis-" is called _______.**
A. a free morpheme　B. a suffix　C. an infix　D. a prefix
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：dis- 加在词根之前，是**前缀(prefix)**，故选 D。A. free morpheme（自由语素）能独立成词，而 dis- 必须附着，属黏着语素；B. suffix（后缀）加在词后，位置错误；C. infix（中缀）插入词内，英语几乎没有，也不符。

**6. Black English is probably the most widespread and most familiar _____ variety of the English language.**
A. regional　B. ethnic　C. social　D. lower class
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：黑人英语是与特定族群相关的**族群/种族(ethnic)**变体，故选 B。A. regional（地域变体）按地区划分；C. social（社会变体）按社会阶层等划分；D. lower class（下层阶级）不是标准的变体分类术语，且以阶级定义黑人英语并不准确。

**7. The pair of words "alive" and "dead" are ______.**
A. gradable antonyms　B. complementary antonyms　C. relational opposites　D. co-hyponym
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：alive/dead 非此即彼、无中间状态、否定一方即肯定另一方，属**互补反义词(complementary antonyms)**，故选 B。A. gradable（等级反义词，如 hot/cold）有程度之分，可比较，可有中间值，不符；C. relational opposites（关系反义词，如 buy/sell）互为前提关系；D. co-hyponym（同下义词）指同属一个上义词的并列词，均不符。

**8. ________ belong(s) to the Indo-European language family.**
A. English　B. German　C. French　D. All of them
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：英语、德语、法语**都**属于印欧语系(Indo-European)，故选 D。A/B/C 各自单独说都正确但不完整，本题需选涵盖全部的 D。

**9. The sentence "Kids like apples" is a _______.**
A. two-place predication　B. three-place predication　C. no-place predication　D. one-place predication
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：谓词分析中，论元(argument)个数决定 predication 类型。"Kids like apples" 含 kids、apples 两个论元，是**二位谓词结构(two-place)**，故选 A。one-place 只有一个论元（如 The baby smiled）；three-place 有三个论元（如 give）；no-place 无论元（无人称句如 It is raining），均不符。

**10. What is the construction of the sentence: "The baby smiled"?**
A. subordinate　B. coordinate　C. exocentric　D. endocentric
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：主谓句(subject-predicate)整体功能不等同于其中任一直接成分，属**离心结构(exocentric)**，故选 C。D. endocentric（向心结构）中整体与中心词分布相同（如修饰语+名词）；A. subordinate（主从）、B. coordinate（并列）指分句间关系，此处是单句主谓，均不符。

## （二）Exercise 1 · 第二组（第1–8题）

**1. F. de Saussure is a(n) _____ linguist.**
A. American　B. Swiss　C. British　D. Russian
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：索绪尔是**瑞士(Swiss)**语言学家，现代语言学奠基人，故选 B。其余国籍均错误。

**2. Which of the following sounds is a voiced bilabial stop?**
A. [p]　B. [m]　C. [b]　D. [t]
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：[b] 是**浊双唇塞音**（声带振动+双唇闭合+完全阻塞），三特征齐全，故选 C。A. [p] 是清双唇塞音（清音）；B. [m] 是双唇鼻音（非塞音）；D. [t] 是清齿龈塞音（部位错、清音），均不符。

**3. Of the "words" listed below, _____ is not an English word.**
A. [spin]　B. [lkbi]　C. [strikt]　D. [desk]
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：[lkbi] 的音位组合违反英语**音位配列规则(phonotactics)**，英语不允许 /lkb/ 这样的辅音串，故不可能是英语词，选 B。A/C/D 的组合均符合英语音节结构。

**4. The affixes which manifest various grammatical relations or grammatical categories such as "-ing, -est" are called _____.**
A. derivational affixes　B. free morphemes　C. inflectional affixes　D. roots
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：-ing、-est 表达时体、比较级等语法范畴，不改变词类，是**屈折词缀(inflectional affixes)**，故选 C。A. derivational（派生词缀）会造出新词/改变词类；B. free morphemes（自由语素）能独立成词，词缀是黏着的；D. roots（词根）是词的核心而非词缀，均不符。

**5. The sentence containing two clauses joined by a linking word is called a ____ sentence.**
A. coordinate　B. simple　C. subordinate　D. embedded
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：两个分句由连接词并列连接，是**并列句(coordinate)**，故选 A。B. simple（简单句）只有一个分句；C. subordinate（从属）指主从关系，一个分句依附另一个；D. embedded（内嵌）指分句嵌入另一分句内部，均不符"并列连接"。

**6. "Words are names or labels for things". This view is called _____ in semantic theory.**
A. mentalism　B. contextualism　C. conceptualism　D. naming theory
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：认为词就是事物的名称/标签，这是**命名论(naming theory)**（柏拉图提出），故选 D。A. mentalism（心灵主义）、C. conceptualism（概念论）强调意义存于心智概念；B. contextualism（语境论）强调意义由语境决定，均与"词=名称"不同。

**7. The semantic relationship between flower and rose is ____.**
A. hyponyms　B. hyponymy　C. co-hyponyms　D. superordinate
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：flower（上义词）与 rose（下义词）之间的**关系**是**下义关系(hyponymy)**，故选 B。注意区分：A. hyponym（下义词）、D. superordinate（上义词）指的是"词"而非"关系"；C. co-hyponyms 指并列的同级下义词（如 rose 与 tulip）。题目问的是"关系"，故选表示关系的 hyponymy。

**8. The words such as handbook and highway are _____.**
A. formed by blending　B. coined by back-formation　C. compound words　D. derivations
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：hand+book、high+way 由两个自由语素直接组合，是**复合词(compound words)**，故选 C。A. blending（混成，如 smog=smoke+fog）取词的部分拼合；B. back-formation（逆构，如 edit←editor）删去词缀；D. derivation（派生）靠加词缀构词，均不符。

## （三）Exercise 2（第1–34题，全部选择题）

**1. Language is a system of arbitrary vocal symbols used for human ______.**
A. contact　B. communication　C. relation　D. community
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：语言的经典定义为"用于人类**交际(communication)**的任意性声音符号系统"，故选 B。A. contact（接触）、C. relation（关系）、D. community（社区）都不是该定义中的固定用词。

**2. — A nice day, isn't it? — Right! I really enjoy the sunlight. What function does it play?**
A. Emotive　B. Phatic　C. Performative　D. Interpersonal
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：谈天气以维持social contact、并无实质信息传递，是**寒暄功能(phatic)**，故选 B。A. emotive（情感功能）表达说话人情绪；C. performative（施为功能）以言行事；D. interpersonal（人际功能）是 Halliday 体系术语，非此处 Jakobson 功能分类的答案。

**3. ______ refers to the actual realization of the ideal language user's knowledge of the rules of his language in utterances.**
A. Performance　B. Competence　C. Langue　D. Parole
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：Chomsky 区分中，规则知识的**实际运用**是**语言运用(performance)**，故选 A。B. competence（语言能力）是内在规则知识本身；C. langue / D. parole 是 Saussure 的术语（语言/言语），题干用 "ideal language user's knowledge of rules" 是 Chomsky 措辞，故选 performance。

**4. Conventionally a ______ is put in slashes (/ /).**
A. allophone　B. phone　C. phoneme　D. morpheme
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：斜线 // 内标写的是**音位(phoneme)**，故选 C。B. phone（音素）和 A. allophone（音位变体）用方括号 [ ] 标写；D. morpheme（语素）属语法层面，不用音标符号。

**5. An aspirated p, an unaspirated p and an unreleased p are ______ of the /p/ phoneme.**
A. analogues　B. tagmemes　C. morphemes　D. allophones
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：同一音位在不同环境中的不同发音变体是**音位变体(allophones)**，送气/不送气/不除阻的 p 都是 /p/ 的变体，故选 D。其余术语与"音位变体"无关。

**6. Which branch of phonetics concerns the production of speech sounds?**
A. Acoustic　B. Articulatory　C. Auditory　D. None
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：研究语音**产生(production)**过程的是**发音语音学(articulatory phonetics)**，故选 B。A. acoustic（声学语音学）研究声波物理属性；C. auditory（听觉语音学）研究语音的感知，均不符。

**7. Which one is different from the others according to places of articulation?**
A. [n]　B. [m]　C. [b]　D. [p]
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：按**发音部位**，[m][b][p] 都是**双唇音(bilabial)**，而 [n] 是**齿龈音(alveolar)**，与众不同，故选 A。

**8. What kind of sounds can we make when the vocal cords are vibrating?**
A. Voiceless　B. Voiced　C. Glottal stop　D. Consonant
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：声带振动产生**浊音(voiced)**，故选 B。A. voiceless（清音）声带不振动；C. glottal stop（喉塞音）是声门完全闭合；D. consonant（辅音）是宽泛类别，非由"声带振动"定义，均不符。

**9. Nouns, verbs and adjectives can be classified as ______.**
A. lexical words　B. grammatical words　C. function words　D. form words
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：名词、动词、形容词含实在词汇意义，是**实词(lexical words)**，故选 A。B. grammatical words = C. function words（语法词/功能词，如介词连词冠词）主要起语法作用；D. form words 是功能词的旧称，均不符。

**10. Morphemes that represent tense, number, gender and case are called ______ morpheme.**
A. inflectional　B. free　C. bound　D. derivational
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：表示时态、数、性、格等语法范畴的是**屈折语素(inflectional)**，故选 A。D. derivational（派生语素）改变词义或词类；B. free（自由）/C. bound（黏着）是按能否独立成词划分，与"表语法范畴"不是同一维度，屈折语素虽也是黏着的，但最贴切的答案是 inflectional。

**11. There are ______ morphemes in the word denationalization.**
A. three　B. four　C. five　D. six
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：de-nation-al-ize-ation 可切分为 **de + nation + al + ize + ation** 五个语素，故选 C。

**12. In English –ise and –tion are called ______.**
A. prefixes　B. suffixes　C. infixes　D. stems
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：-ise、-tion 加在词后，是**后缀(suffixes)**，故选 B。A. prefixes（前缀）在词前；C. infixes（中缀）在词内；D. stems（词干）是加词缀的基础，均不符。

**13. ______ is a way in which new words may be formed from already existing words by subtracting an affix.**
A. affixation　B. back-formation　C. insertion　D. addition
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：靠**删减**误以为是词缀的部分来构词，是**逆构法(back-formation)**（如 editor→edit），故选 B。A. affixation（加缀法）是相反操作；C. insertion、D. addition 都是"添加"，与"减去"矛盾。

**14. The word TB is formed in the way of ______.**
A. acronymy　B. clipping　C. initialism　D. blending
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：TB 取各词首字母、且逐字母读 /ti:bi:/，是**首字母连写/字母缩略(initialism)**，故选 C。A. acronymy（首字母缩略但拼读成词，如 NATO）；B. clipping（截短，如 phone←telephone）；D. blending（混成），均不符。

**15. The words like comsat and sitcom are formed by ______.**
A. blending　B. clipping　C. back-formation　D. acronymy
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：comsat(communication+satellite)、sitcom(situation+comedy) 取两词的一部分拼合，是**混成法(blending)**，故选 A。其余构词法均不符该拼合方式。

**16. All of them are meaningful except for ______.**
A. lexeme　B. phoneme　C. morpheme　D. word
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：**音位(phoneme)**是最小的区别意义的语音单位，本身**不承载意义**，故选 B。A. lexeme（词位）、C. morpheme（语素，最小意义单位）、D. word（词）都是有意义的单位。

**17. The syntactic rules of any language are ______ in number.**
A. large　B. small　C. finite　D. infinite
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：句法规则数量**有限(finite)**，但能生成无限的句子，故选 C。D. infinite（无限）说反了——无限的是句子而非规则；A/B（多/少）表述模糊，非专业答案。

**18. A sentence is considered ______ when it does not conform to the grammatical knowledge in the mind of native speakers.**
A. right　B. acceptable　C. grammatical　D. ungrammatical
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：不符合母语者语法知识的句子是**不合语法的(ungrammatical)**，故选 D。C. grammatical（合语法）意思相反；B. acceptable（可接受）是语用/使用层面概念，不等于合语法；A. right 非术语。

**19. Syntactically, English is an example of ____ language.**
A. VSO　B. SVO　C. SOV　D. OSV
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：英语基本语序是**主-谓-宾(SVO)**，故选 B。其余语序对应其他类型语言（如日语 SOV）。

**20. The phrase "the book on the shelf" belongs to ______ construction.**
A. endocentric　B. exocentric　C. subordinate　D. coordinate
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：该短语整体是名词性的，与中心词 book 分布相同，属**向心结构(endocentric)**，故选 A。B. exocentric（离心结构）整体功能不等于任一成分（如主谓句、介词短语内部）；C/D 指成分间的主从/并列关系，均不符。

**21. The naming theory is advanced by ______.**
A. Plato　B. Bloomfield　C. Geoffrey Leech　D. Firth
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：命名论由古希腊哲学家**柏拉图(Plato)**提出，故选 A。其余人物均非命名论提出者。

**22. — Can I borrow your bike? — You have a bike. The second sentence ______ the first.**
A. is synonymous with　B. is inconsistent with　C. entails　D. presupposes
- **答案：D**
- **解析**："借你的自行车"以"你有自行车"为**前提(presupposes)**，故选 D。C. entail（蕴含）是单向的逻辑推出（真句推真句）；A. synonymous（同义）、B. inconsistent（不相容）均不符两句关系。

**23. ______ is a way in which the meaning of a word can be dissected into meaning components, called semantic features.**
A. Predication analysis　B. Componential analysis　C. Phonemic analysis　D. Grammatical analysis
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：把词义分解为语义特征(semantic features)的方法是**成分分析法(componential analysis)**，故选 B。A. predication analysis（谓词分析）分析句子命题结构；C. phonemic（音位分析）、D. grammatical（语法分析）不涉及语义特征分解。

**24. "Alive" and "dead" are ______.**
A. gradable antonyms　B. relational antonyms　C. complementary antonyms　D. None
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：alive/dead 非此即彼、无程度，是**互补反义词(complementary)**，故选 C。（同 Ex1 第一组第7题）A. gradable 有程度可比较；B. relational 互为关系前提；均不符。

**25. ______ deals with the relationship between the linguistic element and the non-linguistic world of experience.**
A. Reference　B. Concept　C. Semantics　D. Sense
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：语言符号与外部世界之间的关系是**所指/指称(reference)**，故选 A。D. sense（涵义）是词语间的内部意义关系，不涉及外部世界；B. concept（概念）在心智中；C. semantics（语义学）是整个学科，范围过大，均不符"符号—世界"这一关系。

**26. ______ refers to the phenomenon that words having different meanings have the same form.**
A. Polysemy　B. Synonymy　C. Homonymy　D. Hyponymy
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：不同意义共用同一形式是**同形/同音异义(homonymy)**，故选 C。A. polysemy（一词多义）是同一个词有多个相关义项；B. synonymy（同义）；D. hyponymy（下义），均不符。（辨析：homonymy 是不同的词恰好同形；polysemy 是一个词的多义。）

**27. Words that are close in meaning are called ______.**
A. homonyms　B. polysemies　C. hyponyms　D. synonyms
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：意义相近的词是**同义词(synonyms)**，故选 D。其余各项分别指同形词、多义、下义词，均不符。

**28. What essentially distinguishes semantics and pragmatics is whether ______ is considered.**
A. reference　B. speech act　C. practical usage　D. context
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：语义学与语用学的根本区别在于是否考虑**语境(context)**——语用学研究语境中的意义，故选 D。其余选项不是划分二者的根本标准。

**29. A sentence is a ______ concept, and the meaning of a sentence is often studied in isolation.**
A. pragmatic　B. grammatical　C. mental　D. conceptual
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：句子是**语法(grammatical)**概念，可脱离语境孤立研究其意义，故选 B。与之相对，utterance（话语）才是语用概念、须结合语境。A. pragmatic 恰是相反的一端；C/D 非标准表述。

**30. If we think of a sentence as what people actually utter in the course of communication, it becomes a(n) ______.**
A. constative　B. directive　C. utterance　D. expressive
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：交际中实际说出的句子成为**话语(utterance)**，故选 C。A. constative（表述句）、B. directive（指令，言语行为类别）、D. expressive（表情类）都是具体的言语行为/句类，不是"实际说出的句子"这一总概念。

**31. ______ is the act performed by or resulting from saying something; it is the consequence of, or the change brought about by the utterance.**
A. A locutionary act　B. An illocutionary act　C. A perlocutionary act　D. A performative act
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：话语所带来的**效果/后果**是**取效行为(perlocutionary act)**，故选 C。A. locutionary（言内行为）是说出有意义话语本身；B. illocutionary（言外行为）是说话人的意图/用意；D. performative act 非三分行为术语，均不符。

**32. ______ are often thought to be the smallest meaningful units of language by the linguists.**
A. Words　B. Morphemes　C. Phonemes　D. Sentences
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：**语素(morphemes)**是最小的**有意义**语言单位，故选 B。C. phonemes（音位）是最小语音单位但无意义；A. words（词）可再切分为语素；D. sentences（句子）是较大单位，均不符。

**33. All the acts that belong to the same category share the same purpose, but they differ ______.**
A. in their illocutionary acts　B. in their intentions expressed　C. in their strength or force　D. in their effect brought about
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：同类言语行为目的相同，区别在于其**力度/强度(strength or force)**，故选 C。（如"建议"与"命令"目的都是让人做事，但强度不同。）其余选项不是同类内部的区分依据。

**34. The person who is often described as "father of modern linguistics" is ______.**
A. Firth　B. Saussure　C. Halliday　D. Chomsky
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：**索绪尔(Saussure)**被誉为"现代语言学之父"，故选 B。Firth、Halliday 属伦敦学派/系统功能语言学；Chomsky 是转换生成语法创始人，均非该称号所指。

## （四）Exercise 3 · 第一组（第1–15题）

**1. Linguistics is the scientific study of ______.**
A. a particular language　B. the English language　C. Human languages in general　D. the system of a particular language
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：语言学是对**人类语言总体(languages in general)**的科学研究，故选 C。A/B/D 都局限于某一具体语言，而语言学研究的是语言的普遍规律。

**2. The consonant [v] can be described as having the phonetic features: ______.**
A. voiceless, bilabial, stop　B. voiceless, labiodental, fricative　C. voiced, bilabial, stop　D. voiced, labiodental, fricative
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：[v] 是**浊、唇齿、擦音(voiced, labiodental, fricative)**，三特征全对，故选 D。A/B 的 voiceless 错（[v] 是浊音）；A/C 的 bilabial 及 stop 错（[v] 是唇齿擦音，不是双唇塞音）。

**3. The affix "ed" in the word "learned" is known as a(n) ______.**
A. derivational morpheme　B. free morpheme　C. inflectional morpheme　D. free form
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：-ed 表过去时/过去分词，是**屈折语素(inflectional morpheme)**，不改变词类，故选 C。A. derivational 会造新词/改词类；B. free / D. free form 指能独立成词，而词缀是黏着的，均不符。

**4. "My sister will soon be divorced" ______ "My sister is a married woman".**
A. entails　B. contradicts　C. presupposes　D. is synonymous with
- **答案：C**
- **解析**："将要离婚"以"曾是已婚"为**前提(presupposes)**，故选 C。A. entail（蕴含）是逻辑真值推出，此处不成立；B. contradict（矛盾）、D. synonymous（同义）都不符两句关系。

**5. The branch of linguistics that studies how context influences the way speakers interpret sentences is called ______.**
A. semantics　B. pragmatics　C. sociolinguistics　D. psycholinguistics
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：研究**语境**如何影响句子理解的是**语用学(pragmatics)**，故选 B。A. semantics（语义学）研究脱离语境的抽象意义；C. sociolinguistics（社会语言学）研究语言与社会；D. psycholinguistics（心理语言学）研究语言的心理机制，均不符。

**6. Of the three speech acts, linguists are most interested in the ______ because it is identical with the speaker's intention.**
A. locutionary act　B. illocutionary act　C. perlocutionary act　D. constative act
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：与说话人**意图**一致的是**言外行为(illocutionary act)**，也是言语行为理论的研究核心，故选 B。A. locutionary（言内，说出话语本身）；C. perlocutionary（取效，产生的效果）；D. constative act 非三分类术语，均不符。

**7. Speaker A's perlocutionary act ("Too cold here in the room, isn't it?" → B goes to close the doors) is ______.**
A. It's too cold here in the room.　B. I want to complain about the condition of the room.　C. Close all the doors, please.　D. B's going to close all the doors.
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：取效行为(perlocutionary)是话语产生的**实际效果**——B 去关门，故选 D。A 是言内行为（字面所说）；B/C 是言外行为（说话人的用意/请求），均不是"效果"。

**8. A word with several meanings is called ______.**
A. polysemy　B. antonymy　C. homophony　D. hyponymy
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：一个词有多个（相关）意义是**一词多义(polysemy)**，故选 A。B. antonymy（反义）、C. homophony（同音异义）、D. hyponymy（下义）均不符。

**9. The illocutionary point of ______ is to express the psychological state specified in the utterance.**
A. representatives　B. commissives　C. expressives　D. declaratives
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：表达**心理状态**（如高兴、抱歉、感谢）的是**表情类(expressives)**，故选 C。A. representatives（阐述类）陈述命题真假；B. commissives（承诺类）承诺未来行为；D. declaratives（宣告类）以言语改变现实状态，均不符。

**10. The pair of words "vacant" and "occupied" are ______.**
A. gradable opposites　B. relational opposites　C. complementary antonyms　D. Synonyms
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：vacant/occupied 非空即占、无中间态，是**互补反义词(complementary antonyms)**，故选 C。A. gradable（等级反义）可比较有程度；B. relational（关系反义）互为前提；D. synonyms（同义）方向全错。

**11. The words "make" and "bus" are called ______ because they can occur unattached.**
A. derivational morphemes　B. inflectional morphemes　C. bound morphemes　D. free morphemes
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：能独立成词、不必附着的是**自由语素(free morphemes)**，故选 D。C. bound（黏着语素）必须附着于他词；A/B 是词缀（也是黏着的），均不符"unattached"。

**12. "Words are names or labels for thing." This view is called ______ in semantic theory.**
A. mentalism　B. conceptualism　C. naming theory　D. contextualism
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：词=事物名称/标签，是**命名论(naming theory)**，故选 C。（同前）其余理论强调概念、心智或语境，均不符。

**13. The kind to which "girl" and "lass" belong is called ______ synonyms.**
A. stylistic　B. dialectal　C. emotive　D. collocational
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：lass 是苏格兰/英格兰北部**方言**用词，与 girl 构成**方言同义词(dialectal synonyms)**，故选 B。A. stylistic（文体同义，如 kid/child/offspring 正式度不同）；C. emotive（情感/评价色彩不同）；D. collocational（搭配不同），均不符。

**14. What is the meaning relationship between "flower/rose"?**
A. Homonymy　B. Antonymy　C. Polysemy　D. Hyponymy
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：rose 是 flower 的下义词，二者为**下义关系(hyponymy)**，故选 D。A. homonymy（同形异义）、B. antonymy（反义）、C. polysemy（一词多义）均不符。

**15. The level of syntactic representation that exists before movement takes place is termed the ______.**
A. phrase structure　B. surface structure　C. syntactic structure　D. deep structure
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：移位(movement)发生**之前**的句法表征是**深层结构(deep structure)**，故选 D。B. surface structure（表层结构）是移位之后的结果；A/C 非该对立术语。

## （五）Exercise 3 · 第二组（第1–8题）

**1. Chomsky uses the term ______ to refer to the actual realization of a language user's knowledge of the rules of his language in linguistic communication.**
A. langue　B. competence　C. parole　D. performance
- **答案：D**
- **解析**：Chomsky 用**运用(performance)**指规则知识在交际中的实际实现，故选 D。B. competence（能力）是内在知识本身；A. langue / C. parole 是 Saussure 术语，题干指明是 Chomsky 用语，故不选。

**2. The same word may have more than one meaning. This is called ______.**
A. polysemy　B. hyponymy　C. antonymy　D. homonymy
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：**同一个**词有多个意义是**一词多义(polysemy)**，故选 A。D. homonymy 是**不同**词恰好同形（关键区别在"同一个词" vs "不同词"）；B. hyponymy、C. antonymy 均不符。

**3. The utterance "We're already working 25 hours a day, eight days a week" obviously violates the maxim of ______.**
A. quality　B. quantity　C. relation　D. manner
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：一天25小时、一周8天客观上**不可能为真**，违反**质的准则(maxim of quality)**（不说假话/无据之言），故选 A。B. quantity（量）、C. relation（关联）、D. manner（方式）针对信息量、相关性、清晰度，均不符此处"明显失真"的情形。

**4. In English, "un-" and "dis-" are called ______.**
A. prefixes　B. infixes　C. suffixes　D. grammatical words
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：un-、dis- 加在词前，是**前缀(prefixes)**，故选 A。B. infixes（中缀）在词内；C. suffixes（后缀）在词后；D. grammatical words（语法词）是独立的词而非词缀，均不符。

**5. Noam Chomsky is a(n) ______ linguist.**
A. French　B. British　C. American　D. Russian
- **答案：C**
- **解析**：乔姆斯基是**美国(American)**语言学家、转换生成语法创始人，故选 C。其余国籍均错误。

**6. The naming theory was proposed by ______.**
A. the Greek scholar Plato　B. C. K. Ogden and I. A. Richards　C. the British linguist J. Firth　D. the American linguist L. Bloomfield
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：命名论由古希腊学者**柏拉图(Plato)**提出，故选 A。B. Ogden & Richards 提出语义三角；C. Firth 主张语境论；D. Bloomfield 代表行为主义意义观，均非命名论提出者。

**7. "I bought some roses" ______ "I bought some flowers".**
A. Entails　B. Presupposes　C. Is inconsistent with　D. Is synonymous with
- **答案：A**
- **解析**：买了玫瑰**逻辑上必然推出**买了花，是**蕴含(entailment)**，故选 A。（rose 是 flower 的下义词，下义句蕴含上义句。）B. presuppose（预设）、C. inconsistent（不相容）、D. synonymous（同义）均不符。

**8. The description of a language as it changes through time is a ______ study.**
A. comparative　B. diachronic　C. up-to-date　D. descriptive
- **答案：B**
- **解析**：随**时间**变化研究语言是**历时(diachronic)**研究，故选 B。与之相对，共时(synchronic)研究某一时间点。A. comparative（比较）、D. descriptive（描写）不是时间维度的对立术语；C. up-to-date 非术语。

- **答案：morphology（形态学）**
- **解析**：研究构词与词结构的分支是形态学，故填 morphology。

**4. Utterance meaning is based on s________ m________; it is the realization of the abstract meaning of a sentence in a real situation of communication.**
- **答案：sentence, meaning（句子意义 sentence meaning）**
- **解析**：话语意义建立在**句子意义(sentence meaning)**之上，是抽象句义在真实语境中的实现，故填 sentence + meaning。

**5. Hyponymy is the relationship between specific and general lexical items. The word that is more general in meaning is called s________.**
- **答案：superordinate（上义词）**
- **解析**：下义关系中意义较**概括/一般**的词是上义词(superordinate)，较具体的是下义词(hyponym)，故填 superordinate。（原文Key 印作 "superodinate" 为拼写笔误。）

# 三、判断题

> 三份练习中仅 **Exercise 1 第一组** 含判断题（第21–30题，原文缺第22、23题）。

**21. English language is a tone language.**
- **答案：F（错）**
- **解析**：英语是**语调语言(intonation language)**，靠句子语调区别意义；**声调语言(tone language)**（如汉语）才靠字/音节声调区别词义。英语不是声调语言，故为 F。

**24. Phonology is a branch of linguistics which studies the sentence patterns of a language.**
- **答案：F（错）**
- **解析**：研究"句型/句子结构"的是**句法学(syntax)**；**音系学(phonology)**研究的是语言的**语音系统/音位如何组织**。定义张冠李戴，故为 F。

**25. Idiolect is a personal dialect of an individual speaker that combines aspects of all the elements regarding regional, social, and stylistic variation.**
- **答案：T（对）**
- **解析**：individual speaker 独有、综合地域/社会/文体等变异的个人方言正是 idiolect 的定义，表述正确，故为 T。

**26. It is generally accepted that the history of the English language is divided into the periods of Old English and Modern English.**
- **答案：F（错）**
- **解析**：英语史通常分为**三个**时期：古英语(Old English)、中古英语(Middle English)、现代英语(Modern English)。此处漏掉 Middle English，故为 F。

**27. A locutionary act of Speech Act Theory is the act performed by or resulting from saying something.**
- **答案：F（错）**
- **解析**："由说话所产生的效果/后果"是**取效行为(perlocutionary act)**的定义；**言内行为(locutionary act)**指说出有意义话语这一行为本身。定义错配，故为 F。

**28. According to predication analysis, in "The man beat the dog", "beat" is called predicate, and "man" and "dog" are called arguments.**
- **答案：T（对）**
- **解析**：谓词分析中，动词 beat 为谓词(predicate)，man 与 dog 为论元(arguments)，表述正确，故为 T。

**29. [f] is a dental consonant.**
- **答案：F（错）**
- **解析**：[f] 是**唇齿音(labiodental)**（上齿+下唇），不是**齿音(dental)**（如 [θ]，舌尖抵齿）。发音部位错误，故为 F。

**30. The transcription of speech sounds with letter-symbols together with the diacritics is called broad transcription.**
- **答案：F（错）**
- **解析**：字母符号**加上附加符号(diacritics)**的标音是**严式标音(narrow transcription)**；**宽式标音(broad transcription)**只用字母符号、不加附加符号。故为 F。
