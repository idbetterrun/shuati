# 国际收支平衡表 (Balance of Payments) 核心单选复习课

### 一、 国际收支（BOP）基本原理与结构

#### 1. BOP的定义
**题目：** The balance of payments records:
* A. All value flows between residents of a country and non-residents during a period
* B. Trade between a country’s government and foreign governments
* C. Domestic financial transactions of a country
* D. The quantity of imported and exported goods of a country

* **你的答案：** A
* **正确答案：** A
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 国际收支平衡表记录的是特定时期内，一国**居民**与**非居民**之间发生的所有经济价值流量。这是一个典型的**流量（Flow）**概念。

#### 4. 货物与服务差额的通称
**题目：** The goods and services balance is often called:
* A. Capital account balance
* B. Trade balance
* C. Official reserve balance
* D. Financial account balance

* **你的答案：** B
* **正确答案：** B
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 货物与服务差额通常被通称为**贸易差额（Trade balance）**，是经常账户中最核心、最受关注的组成部分。

#### 18. 流量与存量的辨析
**题目：** The international investment position is:
* A. A flow concept
* B. A stock concept
* C. Only records trade
* D. Only records capital flows

* **你的答案：** B
* **正确答案：** B
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** **国际收支（BOP）** 记录的是一段时间内的交易，是**流量**；而**国际投资头寸（IIP）** 记录的是某一特定时点上一国对外资产负债的整体状况，属于**存量（Stock）**。

### 二、 复式记账法的运用原理

#### 2. 贷方（+）的经济含义
**题目：** In double-entry bookkeeping for the balance of payments, a credit item (+) represents:
* A. Items for which the country makes payments abroad
* B. Items for which the country receives payments from abroad
* C. An increase in official reserves
* D. Goods imports

* **你的答案：** B
* **正确答案：** B
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 在 BOP 记账中，**贷方（Credit, +）** 始终代表**资金流入**（本国收到来自境外的款项，例如出口、外资流入）。

#### 5. 理论上的账面总平衡
**题目：** The balance of payments uses double-entry bookkeeping, so the overall balance is always equal to:
* A. Current account balance
* B. Financial account balance
* C. Zero
* D. Changes in official reserves

* **你的答案：** C
* **正确答案：** C
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 因为每一笔交易都是等额的借贷双向记录（有借必有贷），在计入误差与遗漏项之后，BOP 账面的净整体平衡（Overall balance）在逻辑上永远**等于零（Zero）**。

#### 13. 复式记账的对应关系
**题目：** Each transaction in the balance of payments corresponds to:
* A. Only one credit entry
* B. Only one debit entry
* C. One equal debit and one equal credit entry
* D. Only statistical amount

* **你的答案：** C
* **正确答案：** C
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 每一笔国际经济交易都必然对应**一个等额的借方分录和一个等额的贷方分录**。

#### 11. 居民购买外国股票

* **正确答案：** C

#### 14. 外国游客在本国消费

#### 15. 本国向外提供单方面援助
**题目：** Unilateral foreign aid provided by the home country should be recorded as:

#### 16. 投资收益的归属
**题目：** Investment income (dividends, interest) should be recorded in:

#### 17. 外资购买本国政府债券
**题目：** Foreign investors buying domestic government bonds belongs to:
* A. Credit, current account
* B. Credit, financial account
* C. Debit, current account

* **你的答案：** B
* **正确答案：** B
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 购买本国国债属于证券投资负债增加，计入**金融账户（Financial account）**；外资流入本国，记入**贷方（Credit）**。

### 四、 差额的经济含义与官方储备

#### 6. 经常账户逆差意味着什么？
**题目：** A current account deficit means the country is:
* A. A net international lender
* B. A net international borrower
* C. Increasing official reserves
* D. Having exports greater than imports

* **你的答案：** B
* **正确答案：** B
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 经常账户出现逆差（Deficit），意味着该国入不敷出，需要通过向海外借债或者出卖国内金融资产来平衡，因此它在国际上是**净借入者（Net international borrower）**。

#### 20. 经常账户顺差意味着什么？
**题目：** A current account surplus means the country:
* A. Increases net foreign liabilities
* B. Is a net international lender
* C. Has imports greater than exports
* D. Has domestic savings less than investment

* **你的答案：** B
* **正确答案：** B
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 经常账户出现顺差（Surplus），说明贸易和收入获得净流入，有多余的资金向境外投资或放贷，因此是**净贷出者（Net international lender）**。

#### 8. 官方储备资产的排除
**题目：** Official international reserve assets do NOT include:
* A. Gold
* B. Freely convertible foreign currencies
* C. Domestic currency
* D. Special Drawing Rights (SDRs)

* **你的答案：** C
* **正确答案：** C
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 官方储备必须是中央银行持有的、可以直接用于国际清算和干预市场的**境外资产**。**本国货币（Domestic currency）**无法充当本国的国际储备。

#### 12. 官方储备资产的认定
**题目：** Which of the following is an official international reserve asset?
* A. Foreign currency deposits held by domestic enterprises
* B. Gold held by the central bank
* C. Foreign direct investment
* D. Overseas portfolio investment

* **你的答案：** B
* **正确答案：** B
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 只有**中央银行持有的黄金（Monetary Gold）**或外汇才是官方储备。民间企业或个人的资产不能算作官方储备。

#### 19. 官方储备增加的记账方向
**题目：** An increase in official reserves should be recorded as:
* A. Credit
* B. Debit
* C. Not recorded
* D. Statistical discrepancy

* **你的答案：** B
* **正确答案：** B
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 官方储备属于国家持有的金融资产。资产增加意味着资金的占用（买入外汇并存起来），根据会计原理，**资产的增加记在借方（Debit，用负号 "-" 表示）**。

### 五、 综合差额与统计误差

#### 9. 净误差与遗漏的主要原因
**题目：** The main cause of statistical discrepancy in the balance of payments is:
* A. Transactions not occurring

* **你的答案：** B
* **正确答案：** B
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 由于各项数据来自海关、银行、外汇局等不同的统计申报渠道，必然存在时滞、走私逃汇或漏报，因此导致了**记录错误与遗漏（Recording errors and omissions）**。

#### 10. 综合差额（综合结算差额）公式
**题目：** The overall balance (official settlements balance) equals:
* A. Current account + Financial account + Statistical discrepancy
* B. Current account only
* C. Financial account only

* **你的答案：** A
* **正确答案：** A
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** **综合差额（Overall balance）** 涵盖了经常账户、非储备金融账户以及净误差与遗漏的代数和。这个差额对应的对冲项就是官方储备资产的变动。

* A. is defined as the statistical record of a country's international transactions over a certain period of time presented in the form of a double-entry bookkeeping

#### 2. 收到境外款项的记账方向
**题目：** Generally speaking, any transaction that results in a receipt from foreigners:
* A. Will be recorded as a debit, with a negative sign, in the U.S. balance of payments

#### 3. 向境外付款的记账方向

* A. Will be recorded as a debit, With a negative sign, in the U.S. balance of payments

* **正确答案：** A
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 任何导致向外国人**支付款项（Payment）**的交易，都代表资金流出，在 BOP 中记为**借方（Debit）**，并带有**负号（-）**。

#### 5. 复式记账的双向平衡
**题目：** Since the balance of payments is presented as a system of double-entry bookkeeping:

### 四、 宏观经济恒等式与账户联动

#### 12. 国民收入（GNP）核算公式
**题目：** National income, or Gross National Product is given by:
* A. GNP= Y= C+I+G+X+ M
* B. GNP= Y=C+I+G+ X-M
* C. GNP=I=C+Y+G+ X-M
* D. GNP= Y=C+I+X+M-G

* **你的答案：** B
* **正确答案：** B
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 经典的支出法 GNP 公式：$$GNP = C + I + G + (X - M)$$ 即：消费 + 投资 + 政府支出 + 净出口（出口 X 减 进口 M）。

#### 13. 经常账户总差额计算题
**题目：** If a country's merchandise exports exceed its imports by \$50 million, services trade balance is net \$30 million and unilateral transfers made in excess of those received by \$5 million, what is the country's current account balance?
* A. \$85 million
* B. \$75 million
* C. \$15 million
* D. \$25 million

* **你的答案：** B
* **正确答案：** B
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 
  $$\text{Current Account} = \text{Goods Balance} + \text{Services Balance} + \text{Net Transfers}$$
  本题中：商品顺差（+\$50M）+ 服务顺差（+\$30M）+ 单方面转移净流出（-\$5M）= **+\$75 million**。

#### 14. 经常账户逆差的多维宏观图景
**题目：** If the U.S. runs current account deficits, we can expect that:
* A. it may act as a net debtor in the rest of the world
* B. its domestic saving may less than its domestic investment
* C. its domestic production is less than its domestic consumption
* d. all of the above

* **你的答案：** D
* **正确答案：** D
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 经常账户逆差（CA Deficit）在宏观上意味着：① 它是全球的**净债务国（Net debtor）**；② 根据 $S - I = CA$，其**国内储蓄小于国内投资（S < I）**；③ 该国的**国内总产出小于总消费/总吸收（Production < Consumption）**。因此全选。

#### 15. 贸易逆差的直观定义
**题目：** The trade deficit means that:
* A. residents are importing more goods than they are exporting
* B. residents are borrowing more funds than they are lending
* C. residents are receiving more payments than they are making
* D. residents are producing more goods than they are consuming

* **你的答案：** A

#### 16. 储蓄大于投资的宏观结果

### 一、 BOP 核心概念与宏观概念辨析

#### 1. BOP 记录范围的全面性

#### 8. 国际投资头寸（IIP）的时效属性

#### 16. 顺差与经济表现的非绝对关系

### 二、 经常账户 (Current Account) 子项与记账

* **你的答案：** 对 (True)

#### 15. 单方面转移的认定

### 三、 金融账户 (Financial Account) 交易辨析

### 四、 BOP 借贷规则与官方储备记账（最核心考点 🔥）

#### 2 & 11. 商品进出口的记账方向
* **题目2：** Goods imports are debit items in the balance of payments. —— **答案：对 (True)**
* **题目11：** Goods exports are recorded as credits, and goods imports as debits. —— **答案：对 (True)**
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 
  * 商品**出口（Exports）** $\rightarrow$ 资金流入 $\rightarrow$ 记入**贷方（Credit, +）**
  * 商品**进口（Imports）** $\rightarrow$ 资金流出 $\rightarrow$ 记入**借方（Debit, -）**

#### 4. 外国游客在本国消费的记账
**题目：** Foreign tourists’ spending in the home country is a credit item in the home country’s balance of payments.
* **你的答案：** 对 (True)
* **正确答案：** 对 (True)
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 对。外国游客在本国消费属于本国服务的出口，为本国带来外汇资金流入，因此记入本国的**贷方（Credit）**。

#### 7 & 18. 官方储备资产变动的借贷方向（极易混淆 ⚡）
* **题目7：** An increase in official reserves is recorded as a credit in the balance of payments. —— **答案：错 (False)**
* **题目18：** A decrease in official reserves is recorded as a credit. —— **答案：对 (True)**
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 官方储备属于金融**资产**。
  * 储备资产**增加（Increase）** $\rightarrow$ 资金被占用/买入外汇 $\rightarrow$ 记入**借方（Debit, -）**
  * 储备资产**减少（Decrease）** $\rightarrow$ 释放外汇储备变现/资金流出资产端 $\rightarrow$ 记入**贷方（Credit, +）**

#### 10. 综合逆差与官方储备的对冲
**题目：** An overall balance deficit is usually financed by a decrease in official reserve assets.
* **你的答案：** 对 (True)
* **正确答案：** 对 (True)
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 对。当一国面临综合差额逆差（Overall deficit）时，意味着资金净流出，此时中央银行通常需要动用外汇储备进行干预（**减少官方储备资产**，在贷方记正号），来弥补和对冲这个缺口。

#### 6. 经常账户与国际净投资的等式
**题目：** Current account surplus = positive net foreign investment.
* **你的答案：** 对 (True)
* **正确答案：** 对 (True)
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 对。经常账户顺差意味着该国拥有净国民储蓄盈余，在宏观上必然等于其对外净投资额（Net Foreign Investment 或 Net Capital Outflow）。

#### 13. 复式记账的账面平衡恒等式
**题目：** The sum of all items in the balance of payments is always zero.
* **你的答案：** 对 (True)
* **正确答案：** 对 (True)
* 💡 **AI 核心解析：** 对。由于严格的复式记账和净误差与遗漏项的调节，BOP 平衡表上所有项目的借贷代数和在账面上**总是等于零**。
